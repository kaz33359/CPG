OTHM Level 6 Diploma in Occupational Health and Safety

  • Best Seller

Overview

Course Description

OTHM qualifications are approved and regulated by Ofqual, (Office of the Qualifications and Examinations Regulation) learners are eligible to progress to top-up degree and masters programmes at many universities in UK and overseas with advanced standing.

Advantages of OTHM Qualifications:

• IOSH (UK) GradIOSH Membership:

OTHM Level 6 Diploma holders are eligible for Graduate Level Membership with IOSH (UK) – GradIOSH and then Plan their CPD & IPD for attaining Chartered Membership status “CMIOSH” with IOSH, UK in future.

    The Salient features of Ofqual Accredited OTHM Level 6 Diploma in OHS are:

  • UK Government body Ofqual accredited Level 6 Diploma.

  • This qualification is equivalent to Graduation / Honours Degree Qualification in OHS

  • NO Exams, Only assignments are needed to be submitted for each Unit

  • Easiest and Quickest way to achieve GradIOSH membership.

    DURATION:

  • 4-6 Months
