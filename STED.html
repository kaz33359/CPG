Certification Body

Scientific & Technical Education Development Council (STED COUNCIL) is an Autonomous Body and a National Level Organization registered under Govt. of Kerala and Indian NGO under National Capital Territory (Govt. of N.C.T) New Delhi, Government of India. STED council is appropriately registered and approved from the relevant state and central government bodies who have been awarded the prestigious, according to ISO 9001:2008 standard which certifies by QCert. international certification body Czech Republic, Europe which provide acceptance of worldwide and has been awarded an international graded quality assurance certification by the very renowned international body DAC-Dubai accreditation center (recognized in Middle East) & under accredited unit JAS-ANZ (Govt. appointed joint accreditation system of New Zealand & Australia)
